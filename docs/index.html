Introduction

Real World Problem

In todayâ€™s globalized world, families and friends are often separated by physical distance.

  • Lack of Shared Activities: Reduced opportunities for fun and interactive experiences.
  • Emotional Disconnect: Weaker relationships and feelings of loneliness.
  • Missed Memories: Missing out on special moments and bonding opportunities.

Proposed Solution

Creating a VR multiplayer golf game that brings families and friends closer.

How it works

  • Players use VR headsets and motion sensors on their hands and golf sticks to play a realistic game of golf.
  • A multiplayer mode allows users to join virtual game rooms, interact via voice chat, and enjoy the game together.
  • The game includes different difficulty levels, making it suitable for beginners and experienced players alike.

Potential Impact

Positively impact society by bridging long-distance relationships, promoting physical and mental well-being, and making golf more accessible and affordable. It fosters social inclusion by allowing people of all ages and abilities to participate, while also addressing issues of sedentary lifestyles and social isolation. Economically, it provides a cost-effective alternative to traditional golf and creates new opportunities in the VR and e-sports industries. Technologically, it advances VR adoption and innovation in gamified fitness, contributing to a more connected and health-conscious society.

Testing

We are going to test the system using two sets of hardware and software configurations to ensure compatibility and performance. The first set will include a high-end VR headset with advanced motion sensors, while the second set will use a mid-range VR headset with basic motion sensors. Both sets will be tested under the same conditions to evaluate the system's performance, responsiveness, and user experience.
