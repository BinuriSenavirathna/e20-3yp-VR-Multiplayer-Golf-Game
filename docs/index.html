VR Multiplayer Golf Game

Compete, socialize, and perfect your swing in an immersive virtual world!

About

Enjoy an immersive golf experience from the comfort of your home.

Real World Problem

  • Lack of Shared Activities: Reduced opportunities for fun and interactive experiences.
  • Emotional Disconnect: Weaker relationships and feelings of loneliness.
  • Missed Memories: Missing out on special moments and bonding opportunities.

Proposed Solution

Creating a VR multiplayer golf game that brings families and friends closer.

How it Works

  • Players use VR headsets and motion sensors on their hands and golf sticks to play a realistic game of golf.
  • A multiplayer mode allows users to join virtual game rooms, interact via voice chat, and enjoy the game together.
  • The game includes different difficulty levels, making it suitable for beginners and experienced players alike.

Potential Impact

This project aims to bridge long-distance relationships, promote physical and mental well-being, and make golf more accessible. It fosters social inclusion, tackles sedentary lifestyles, and contributes to the VR and e-sports industries.

Implementation

Details about how our VR golf game is developed.

Hardware Stack

We use VR headsets, motion sensors, and haptic feedback devices to simulate a realistic golf experience.

Data Flow Stack

Real-time data synchronization between players ensures smooth multiplayer interactions.

Tech Stack

Our game is built using Unity 3D, C#, and optimized physics engines to enhance gameplay realism.

AWS

We leverage AWS for scalable cloud storage, multiplayer matchmaking, and real-time game hosting.
