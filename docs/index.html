Introduction

The VR gaming industry is growing rapidly, but the VR golf segment faces challenges:

  • Social Isolation: Lack of multiplayer features and real-time interaction.
  • High Costs: Premium VR games are expensive and inaccessible in regions like Sri Lanka.
  • Limited Accessibility: Busy lifestyles prevent casual gamers from experiencing golf.
